# EP-05 — Team Assignment & Availability

**Epic ID:** EP-05
**Title:** Team Assignment and Availability View
**PRD Reference:** Section 7.4, Section 7.5
**Priority:** P2
**Status:** Not Started

---

## Goal

Allow management to assign employees to projects, see current team composition, identify domain knowledge gaps, and view an organisation-wide availability bench.

---

## Scope

- Assign and remove employees from a project
- Show role and relevant domain knowledge per assignment
- Flag domain knowledge mismatch — project requires domain no team member has
- Availability view — all employees with Assigned / Available / Partially Available status
- Partially Available — set by PM as a flag on an assignment
- Filter availability view by domain and availability status

---

## Out of Scope

- Employee entity creation (EP-02)
- Timesheet or hours tracking (out of scope for v1.0 per PRD)
